About Me

Namaste! I'm, Aryan Kaushik, I'm a full stack developer currently studying computer science and engineering. I love technology and programming. In my free time I either work on my projects or do an online course because I also love to learn new stuff.
All my projects started either with others wanting something I did or me helping non-developers get features they wanted without coding themselves.
I’ve also completed several courses, including but not limited to - CS50, CS50w, Google’s crash course on python, Google IT support specialization, etc. These courses helped me in learning new languages and things I had no idea about before.
I also qualified for GSoC'22 under GNOME Foundation and Pitivi project.
I like to work with others in accomplishing a common goal and I also love to help newcomers and share my knowledge.
My goal is to keep contributing to meaningful projects, to give back to the community by sharing my knowledge.
I code because I love to.
